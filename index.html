About Me

Hey, I'm Richard a frontend dev from Leeds, England. I have spent the last few years travelling the world, before settling down first in Kraków, Poland & then in Oslo, Norway.

I first started building web pages at 14 and later refound my passion in 2016. Working at Tagvenue as employee #3, I gotto see what it was like to take a start-up from idea, to venue search engine on the web.

Moving to Oslo in May 2016, I started to focus more on freelance work and worked with Oslo start-up Growthitude, and local businesss website provider vylocal.com.
